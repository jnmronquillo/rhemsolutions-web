OUR PROJECTS

Rhem solutions we are a Peruvian startup especializada focused in tailor made software that can accommodate customer's preferences and expectations. Some use cases, include content management, inventory management, customer management, human resource management, or otherwise to fill the gaps present in the existing legacy software.

Some of our projects are:

IMAGE MANAGEMENT (CLOUDIMG).

Manages documents in image format, in a simple way, allows you upload large ammount of images to S3 using a desktop client developed in python, Search and download the image individually from the Web App, and has three types o users: Administrator, Operator and Final User.

POINT OF SALE (POSWEB)

Is a small web app for clothing stores, you can load stock, send clothes to other point of sale, and generate reports like, sales report, stock of clothes by store, etc.

EXPENSE MANAGER (SIPAGA).

Manages expenses of project auditors about the enterprises being audited.

EXPENSE MANAGER, MATERIALS AND CONTRATAS (SIGMAC).

Manages expenses , materials and contratas for Ericsson Perú.
